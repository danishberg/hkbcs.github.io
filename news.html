HKBCS President among finalists in HKMA and BIS Trade Finance Challenge

The Hong Kong Blockchain Society would like to congratulate our President Dr. Lawerence Ma and his team on being shortlisted from a total of 103 finalists at the Global Trade Technology Challenge. This TechChallenge is a joint initiative of the BIS Innovation Hub (BISIH) and the Hong Kong Monetary Authority (HKMA), and is designed to showcase the potential for new innovative technologies to resolve problems in trade finance (TradeTech). It is supported by the Asian Development Bank (ADB), the International Chamber of Commerce (ICC), the International Institute of Finance (IIF), the People's Bank of China (PBOC) and the Wolfsberg Group.
